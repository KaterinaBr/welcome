Transport

Tickets

This card is for you

You buy it at the Issuing Points in every metro station once and you can top it up again - so don’t throw them!
You can use it to enter in the metro lines, buses and trolleys.
- Normal ticket: 1.40 euros / 90 minutes
- Discount ticket: 0.60 euros / 90 minutes
- 24-hour ticket: 4.5 euros
- 5-day ticket: 9 euros
- etc.

This card is for citizens

The card has its owner name.
You can pay per month/year etc.

Street Shopping

Periptero

Photo by www.greece-is.com

Periptero is a small kiosk placed randomly on the sidewalks of Greece.
In these kiosks you can buy cigaretes, gums, magazines, snacks, ice-creams, and many many more things.
You are out of chocolate? Oh yes, there is a periptero there, on the corner!

Laiki Agora

Laiki Agora is a flea market full of in season fruits and vegetables.
These flea markets are in a specific neighbourhoods around Athens, weekly.
For example, at Perissos, there is a flea market every Saturday.
There, you can also find fresh fish, wine, olive oil, spices, flowers...

Trash Bins

There are 2 basic types of rubbish bins.

The first type is the green or silver bins.
Here, you can throw away every kind of trash - except the recyclable ones!

Second type, blue bins.
This is for recycling paper, metal, plastic, glass.
